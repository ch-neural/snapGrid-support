SnapGrid 隱私權政策

Last updated: 2026-01-27

資料使用 / Data Usage

SnapGrid 需要存取相簿以讓使用者選取照片與儲存拼貼結果。照片只在裝置端處理，不會上傳到我們的伺服器。

SnapGrid accesses your photo library to select photos and save your collage. Photos are processed on-device and are not uploaded to our servers.

廣告 / Ads

我們使用 Google AdMob 顯示廣告。AdMob 可能會收集裝置資訊（如裝置識別碼、IP 位址）以提供廣告服務與衡量成效。

We use Google AdMob for ads. AdMob may collect device information (such as device identifiers and IP address) for ad delivery and measurement.

詳細資訊請參考： Google Privacy Policy
